Happy Planet Index

 

* The inequalities between people within a country, in terms of how long they live, and how happy they feel, based on the distribution in each country's life expectancy and wellbeing data. Inequality of outcomes is expressed as a percentage.
* How satisfied the residents of each country say they feel with life overall, on a scale from zero to ten, based on data collected as part of the Gallup World Poll.
HPI index
This visualization shows which countries are happiest according to HPI data. The Happiness Index (visualized in the map) is measured with several variables: Wellbeing, lifeExpectancy, Inequality of Outcomes and Ecological footprint. The map shows the world coloured on HPI index (happy=yellow, less happy=red). When hovering over the map the HPIrank is shown and the corresponding dot will light up in the scatterplot. This makes it easy to compare lifeExpectancy and wellbeing or inequality with the HPI score. To read more on how the HPI is calculated visit: http://happyplanetindex.org/
